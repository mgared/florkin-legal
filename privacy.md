# Florkin Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 14, 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how Florkin ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses,
and shares your information when you use the Florkin iOS application (the
"App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described below. If you do
not agree, please do not use the App.

## 1. Who we are

Florkin is operated by Misael Gared ("we"). Questions about this policy can be
sent to **misaelgared@gmail.com**.

## 2. Information we collect

We only collect what is necessary to run the App. We do not sell your personal
information and we do not use it for advertising.

### Information you give us directly

- **Account information** — email address, password (if you sign up with
  email), display name, first and last name.
- **Profile content** — avatar (uploaded photo or DiceBear seed), important
  dates (birthdays, anniversaries), goals, habits, income/expense/savings
  entries, household chores, shopping items, and notes you add.
- **Relationship data** — the people you add to your orbit (family, friends,
  lover), and any connection or co-organizer requests you send or accept.
- **Location** (only when you opt in) — your approximate or precise location
  is stored only while you have **Location Sharing** enabled in Settings and
  is visible only to the audience groups or specific users you authorize.

### Information collected automatically

- **Device token** — Apple Push Notification token / Firebase Cloud Messaging
  token, used solely to deliver in-app notifications.
- **Authentication metadata** from Apple, Google, or Firebase (e.g. last
  sign-in date) — used to keep your account secure.

We do **not** collect contacts, microphone audio, advertising identifiers, or
analytics about your activity in the App.

## 3. How we use information

- To provide core features: orbits, dates, goals, habits, money tracking,
  households, shared events, shared savings, and the home/lock-screen widgets.
- To deliver notifications you have asked for (connection requests, shared
  date invitations, shared goal invitations, nudges, reminders).
- To enable optional location sharing with the people you choose.
- To authenticate you on each launch (Apple Sign-In, Google Sign-In, or
  email/password via Firebase Authentication).
- To respond to support requests.

## 4. How we share information

- **With people you choose.** Dates, goals, habits, and savings goals are
  shared only with the audience groups (Family, Friends, House Members) or
  specific users you select. Your location is shared only with users you
  explicitly authorize.
- **With our service provider, Google Firebase.** Florkin stores account and
  app data in Firebase Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, and Firebase
  Cloud Messaging. Firebase processes data on our behalf subject to their
  [terms](https://firebase.google.com/terms) and
  [privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **When required by law** or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do **not** sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

## 5. Data retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your
account at any time in **Settings → Delete Account**; we permanently remove
your profile, people entries, dates, goals, habits, money records, household
data, locations, connection records, and authentication record. Cloud
Functions logs may retain anonymized request metadata for up to 30 days for
debugging.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- Access the information we hold about you.
- Correct inaccurate information (use **Settings → Edit Profile** for the
  most common changes).
- Delete your account and associated data.
- Opt out of location sharing at any time.
- Withdraw consent for push notifications via iOS Settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email **misaelgared@gmail.com**.

## 7. Children's privacy

Florkin is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has
provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

## 8. International transfers

Florkin's backend runs on Google Firebase. Data may be processed in countries
other than the one you live in. By using the App you consent to this transfer.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be
announced in-app or via email. The "Last updated" date above always reflects
the current version.

## 10. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints? Email **misaelgared@gmail.com**.
