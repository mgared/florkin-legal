# Florkin Support

Need help with Florkin? We're here.

## Contact

- **Email:** misaelgared@gmail.com
- **Response time:** within 2 business days

When reporting an issue, please include:

- Your iPhone model and iOS version (e.g. "iPhone 16 Pro, iOS 26.4.1").
- The Florkin version (Settings → bottom row, e.g. "Version 1.0 (1)").
- A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened.
- Screenshots if helpful.

## Common questions

**My widget shows "No lover yet" but I have a lover.**
Open Florkin and visit the Tasks tab. The widget refreshes on app foreground
within ~10 seconds. If your lover is a connected Florkin user, no other step
is required.

**My lover can't see my location.**
In Settings → Location Sharing make sure (1) the master toggle is on,
(2) **Location** permission is granted to Florkin in iOS Settings, and
(3) the **Lover** toggle on the same screen is on. They will see your most
recent location the next time they open your profile or refresh their Lover
widget.

**How do I delete my account?**
Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes all your data.

**I forgot my password.**
On the sign-in screen, enter your email then tap "Forgot password?".
